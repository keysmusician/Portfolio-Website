About

I'm a software engineer. I studied software development and engineering with a specialization in machine learning at Holberton School (now District Arts and Education). Since graduation, I've continued to study machine learning, explore new programming languages, and satisfy my curiosity with topics like digital signal processing, blockchain, and cyber security. I've built projects in a variety of programming languages including C, C#, and TypeScript, with my strongest language being Python.

Some of my projects include an Airbnb clone, a web extension to assist with starting school projects, and a memory training application to help me learn new skills, inspired by the way I teach my piano students.

Prior to studying software development, I was a self-employed piano teacher. When I wasn't teaching, I was producing music, practicing jazz piano, and working personal software projects.

I had been fascinated with computers since I was a kid, and I've always liked to know how things worked under the hood. I had fun completing many of the original Codecademy lessons, and got my first experience with Python in high school when I learned how to write a platformer using PyGame and wrote a Pong clone.

Holberton School had a lot of interesting qualities that made it an attractive choice for my education. It was an incredibly rigorous program; I was challenged on a daily basis. The challenging and collaborative environment developed my ability to work with others, to persevere, and to know my own limits. I also honed soft skills that will benefit me throughout my professional career such as written and public communication, virtual and face-to-face collaboration, online learning and self-study skills.

Whether composing music or writing software, I appreciate being able to realize an idea. It's satisfying to create something with real impact and to know that it's making the world a better place for not only myself, but hopefully many others. I don't just like to get stuff done, but to get it done right. My organizational skills, persistence, love for learning, and attention to detail would be an asset to any team.
